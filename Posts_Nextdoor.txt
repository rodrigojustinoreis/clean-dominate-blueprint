Pro Cleaning Tip: The secret to crystal clear shower glass 🚿

Hi neighbors! Rodrigo from Capital Clean Care here. I see a lot of people struggling with hard water stains on glass shower doors. 

Quick Pro-Tip: Mix equal parts white vinegar and Dawn dish soap in a spray bottle. Spray it on the glass, let it sit for 15 minutes, and scrub gently with a non-scratch sponge. Rinse it off and squeegee it dry. It works like magic! ✨
